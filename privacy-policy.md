# Privacy Policy for Rollr

Last updated: `2026-03-17`

## Who we are

`Rollr` is made by `Fern Drop Studios`.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact:

- Email: `FernDropStudios@gmail.com`

## What Rollr does

`Rollr` is a board game companion app. It includes tools such as dice, timers, coin flips, and similar helper features for tabletop and party games.

## Information we collect

We try to collect as little personal information as possible.

### Information stored on your device

`Rollr` may save app settings and gameplay preferences on your device so the app works properly. For example, this can include:

- favourites
- pinned tools
- renamed items
- custom game or tool settings

This information is stored locally on your device.

### Advertising data

If ads are shown in the app, we may use `Google AdMob` to display them.

AdMob may collect or receive information such as:

- device identifiers
- IP address
- approximate location
- app usage information
- ad performance and interaction data

This information may be used by Google and its partners to provide, improve, and measure ads.

You can read more about how Google uses data here:

- https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

## Information we do not intentionally collect

We do not intentionally ask for or collect:

- your name
- your home address
- your phone number
- your contacts
- your photos
- your microphone recordings
- your camera data

unless a future version of the app clearly tells you otherwise.

## How we use information

We use information to:

- make the app work
- remember your settings and preferences
- improve app stability and performance
- show ads, if ads are enabled

## Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

If advertising is enabled, data may be shared with advertising providers such as `Google AdMob` in order to serve and measure ads.

## Data retention

Local app settings usually remain on your device until you remove them, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

Third-party services such as Google AdMob may keep data according to their own policies.

## Children's privacy

`Rollr` is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will review the issue.

## Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the app simple and limit unnecessary data collection. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

## Your choices

You can:

- stop using the app at any time
- uninstall the app
- clear app data through your device settings

Depending on your region, you may also be offered ad consent choices when ads are shown.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
